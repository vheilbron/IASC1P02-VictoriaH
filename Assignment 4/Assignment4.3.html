Recipe for Banana Chocolate Chip Bread (or Muffins):



I've always had a love for banana bread (especially when there are chocolate chips). With the soft interior and somewhat crunchy exterior, this banana bread is to die for! If you are craving banana bread (and now that you're reading this you probably are) try this recipe! Great for family gatherings, hanging out with friends, or just for yourself!!

What You Will Need:

  • 1 stick of unsalted butter at room temperature (1/2 cup)
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 overripe medium sized bananas, mashed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup dark/ milk chocolate chips (or just semi-sweet)
  • 9x5 loaf pan or muffin pan

Baking Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Line a 9x5 loaf pan with parchment paper, making sure the parchment is hanging over the sides of the loaf pan. This will help you easily remove the loaf after baking. Spray with nonstick spray and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar together for about 2 minutes until smooth. Whisk in the egg then add the mashed bananas and vanilla. Mix, scraping down the sides of the bowl, until well combined.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix until just combined. Careful not to over mix. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Pour mixture into loaf pan or muffin pan and place in oven for 35-45 minutes (depending on your oven) or until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
